Featured Projects

Vibes Social Network

Vibes Social Network addresses the challenge of choosing nightlife venues, developed through extensive user research to resolve key customer issues. The design process involved use of information architecture and wireframes, refining the user experience across multiple iterations. It was recognized at the Algonquin College Applied Project Reaction Showcase for its innovative approach to enhancing social experiences.

Map displays how many people are in each venue

Venue page displays live data reflecting the current atmosphere in venue

BarKeep

BarKeep is a specialized app empowering cocktail enthusiasts and mixologists to craft, catalog, and personalize their unique recipes. Users can add ingredients, select tools used, and infuse old favourites with new creativity, making it perfect for both refining existing classics and innovating new creations.

Home page displays user created cocktails

Cocktail page shows ingredients, equipment required, and instructions

Harmony Haven

Harmony Haven solves the challenge of finding at-home caregivers for the elderly. Built through extensive user testing, the app addresses key issues faced by families and seniors. It connects users with reliable, vetted caregivers, ensuring peace of mind and quality care for loved ones in the comfort of their homes.

Easy to navigate list of caregivers with ratings

Profile features contact information, specialized areas, and qualifications
